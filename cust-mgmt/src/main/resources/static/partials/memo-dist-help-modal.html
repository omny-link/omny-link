Help for Distribution Centre

The distribution centre allows you to manage notifications across organisations and confirm they are acknowledged.

How can we help you today?

Common questions that people have include:

  • How can I use a previous distribution notification again?

    In the list of distributions click to duplicate an existing distribution notification

  • How can I monitor acknowledgments of distribution notifications?

    Once the distribution notification has been sent, a list of Activities will appear below the Distribution showing the individuals that have been contacted and whether they have each acknowledged the distribution or not.
